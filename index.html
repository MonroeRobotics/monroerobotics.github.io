The MonroeBot

Because the game has junctions of different heights, we needed a way to move cones from the ground up to the highest junction. This mechanism needed to be quick and compact (to allow us to have a smaller drivetrain). The lift system ended up being the most iterated design because of the many reliability issues we faced with it.

Our Team

We are 17774 Monroe Robotics from William Monroe High School in Stanardsville, Virginia. Being from one of the smallest counties in Virginia, the STEM field is not as widespread largely due to a lack of urban development, however this also allows us greater opportunities to influence our county. Our team is composed of students ranging from grades 9-12. Our main focus is to help spread and make STEM more accessible to the people in our school and our community. We strive to explore and push the bounds of our team to help develop new skills and foster a passion for engineering and innovation.
