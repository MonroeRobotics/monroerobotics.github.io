Meet the

MonroeBot

Current Year

CenterStage (2023-2024)

In the front of the robot, there is a well designed intake utilizing some spinner thingies to grab pixels and hurl them into the bucket. After the pixels are in the bucket, they get detected by 2 color sensors which present the color onto the controllers for the drivers. Once the drivers give the command, the bucket gets swung over and lifted to the right height to drop off the pixels at the backboard.
